# Sachet - Transparency Platform for Nepali Governance

A modern, citizen-centric prototype designed to bridge the trust gap between the government and the people of Nepal.

**Sachet** is built with a single mission: to make governance transparent and accessible. In a post-GenZ protest landscape, the demand for accountability in Nepal has never been higher. This platform empowers citizens by:
- **Tracking Budgets**: Real-time visualization of how public funds are allocated.
- **Monitoring Projects**: Status updates on infrastructure and local development.
- **Simplified Laws**: Breaking down complex legal documents into easy-to-read summaries.
- **Anonymous Reporting**: A secure, encrypted way for citizens to report corruption without fear of retaliation.
